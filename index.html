Guarantee?

Yes, we offer a guarantee to all our clients. We’ve never had to use it, BUT yes — we have a 100% money-back guarantee. If you commit to everything we provide and still don’t get results, we’ll refund all your money. No questions asked.

Commitment

Inside Fitgenious, we like to work with long-term commited clients that are willing to work together for 3–6 months, and even more.

I’m already posting content, how is this different?

Perfect, that shows you’re already a committed person to grow your brand. We will make your strategy clear, better, and save you time and resources by taking your mind, time, and energy out of the content creation process so you can focus on growing and scaling your business.

What if I’ve tried other content courses or agencies before?

We get it, and that’s why inside FitGenious we only open spots for 5 clients to ensure premium quality. Also, that’s why we have the guarantee. I believe if you decide to work with us, we won’t need to use it, but if needed, we’ll give your money back.

No, you don’t. That’s a goal that we define and work on when working together. You can start with a brand-new account.

What if I don’t have much time to create content?

You probably have the time, you’re just not seeing content as a priority right now. But our team makes it easier and effortless for you to create content without spending more than 3 hours a month on it.

Do you Only work with Fitness coaches?

Yes, we only work with online fitness coaches, because that’s our specialty and focus. We don’t work with 'business owners' who aren’t fitness coaches, nor with in-person trainers who want to start their personal brand.
